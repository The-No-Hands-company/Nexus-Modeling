# v0.16 Planning — ReSTIR PT, Chromatic Aberration/Film Grain, Screen-Space Caustics

Status: **Planning**. Three passes completing the advanced optical rendering stack.

Reference: [ROADMAP.md](../ROADMAP.md), [v0.15-planning.md](v0.15-planning.md).

---

## Motivation

v0.15 delivered ReSTIR GI, cascaded VSM blending, and lens flare. Three optical passes
remain to complete the rendering pipeline:

1. **ReSTIR PT** — path-tracing variant of ReSTIR for specular and caustic paths;
   extends the v0.15 ReSTIR GI reservoir framework to handle full-spectrum indirect
   transport. Explicitly deferred from v0.15.
2. **Chromatic Aberration & Film Grain** — two correlated post-process effects that
   complete the lens simulation pipeline established in v0.15: radial RGB channel
   separation (chromatic aberration) and temporal noise overlay (film grain).
3. **Screen-Space Caustics** — projected caustic pattern from refractive geometry
   (water, glass) reconstructed from the depth buffer and environment map; composited
   additively into the lighting buffer before tone mapping.

## Out of Scope for v0.16

- Full spectral dispersion in ReSTIR PT (hero wavelength sampling — deferred to v0.17).
- Photon-mapped caustics (3-D photon map — deferred to v0.17).
- Adaptive film grain density based on exposure.

---

## Exit Criteria

1. `enableReSTIRPT` triggers path tracing dispatch; `restirPTActive` and
   `restirPTPathCount` populated.
2. `enableCameraLens` triggers aberration/grain passes; `cameraLensActive` and
   `cameraLensPassCount` populated.
3. `enableCaustics` triggers caustic projection; `causticsActive` and
   `causticsSampleCount` populated.
4. All flags default to off — no behavior change for existing callers.
5. All new Null-backend tests pass (target: +24 tests).
6. Null-backend full suite at 100%.
