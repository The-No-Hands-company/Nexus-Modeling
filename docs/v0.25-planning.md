# v0.25 Planning — Deformable Surface Tracking, Varifocal Holographic Stacking, NeRF Relighting

Status: **Planning**. Three passes completing the advanced reconstruction and display
stack deferred from v0.24.

Reference: [ROADMAP.md](../ROADMAP.md), [v0.24-planning.md](v0.24-planning.md).

---

## Motivation

v0.24 delivered neural scene flow, eyebox exit-pupil steering, and NeRF-in-the-wild.
Three items were explicitly deferred:

1. **Deformable Surface Tracking from Neural Scene Flow** — derives a mesh-level
   deformation field from the neural scene flow volume; enables temporally consistent
   deformable mesh tracking aligned to dynamic scene geometry.
2. **Varifocal Holographic Display Stacking** — composites multiple holographic depth
   layers at varying focal distances; produces a varifocal light-field stack for
   near-eye holographic displays.
3. **NeRF Relighting / Material Decomposition** — decomposes a NeRF radiance field into
   diffuse albedo, specular, and roughness layers; enables relighting with novel
   illumination environments.

## Exit Criteria

1. `enableDeformableSurface` triggers deformation dispatch; `deformableSurfaceActive`
   and `deformableSurfaceVertexCount` populated.
2. `enableVarifocalHolographic` triggers focal-layer stack dispatch;
   `varifocalHolographicActive` and `varifocalHolographicLayerCount` populated.
3. `enableNeRFRelighting` triggers material decomposition + IBL dispatch;
   `neRFRelightingActive` and `neRFRelightingSampleCount` populated.
4. All flags default to off — no behavior change for existing callers.
5. All new Null-backend tests pass (target: +24 tests).
6. Null-backend full suite at 100%.
